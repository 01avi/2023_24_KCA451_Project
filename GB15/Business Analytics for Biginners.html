Description

Welcome to Business Analytics for Beginners! This course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive introduction to the field of business analytics and equip you with the skills and knowledge necessary to analyze data and make informed business decisions.

Whether you're new to the field or looking to enhance your existing skills, this course will guide you through the fundamental concepts and techniques of business analytics. You'll learn how to collect, clean, and prepare data for analysis, and explore descriptive, inferential, and predictive analytics techniques.

  • The course covers popular tools and software used in business analytics, including Microsoft Excel and Power BI.
  • Participants will learn data mining and machine learning techniques to extract patterns and insights from data.
  • The course emphasizes the importance of effective communication of findings and recommendations in business analytics.
  • Participants will learn how to visualize data effectively and tell compelling stories with data.
  • By the end of the course, participants will have a strong foundation in business analytics./li>
